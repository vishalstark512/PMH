\caption{\textbf{FGSM robustness (updated) and scale universality.}
\emph{Left:} Updated FGSM results after resolving a non-deterministic first run
(\texttt{cudnn.benchmark=True}). E1~(PMH) achieves 45.30\% at $\varepsilon{=}4/255$
vs.\ paper 48.09\% ($-2.79$\,pp, within single-seed variance).
PMH matches or exceeds VAT without adversarial training at every budget.
VAT collapses at $\varepsilon{=}4/255$ (23.61\%) while PMH achieves 45.30\%:
an incidental robustness benefit of geometry repair.
\emph{Right:} Blind spot ratio (paraphrase drift / non-paraphrase drift) remains below 1.0
across 66M, 110M, and 340M parameter models, confirming the geometric blind spot predicted
by Theorem~1 is a property of the ERM objective, not of scale.}
